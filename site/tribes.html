The Spasm network consists of independent self-hosted interoperable instances run by DAOs, local communities, web3 event organizers, and other freedom seekers who want to escape censorship and surveillance of centralized platforms.

Join the degen tribe. As the first Spasm instance, active since 2021, this community curates news from many RSS sources, keeping the tribe informed and engaged with the latest DeFi, NFT, privacy, and libertarian news.
